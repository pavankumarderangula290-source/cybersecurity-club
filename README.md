# Cybersecurity Club Setup and Rules

The repository serves as a central resource for your cybersecurity club, documenting the club's organization, guidelines, and operational procedures. The README outlines:

**Setup Components:**
- Club structure and hierarchy
- Onboarding procedures for new members
- Initial configuration and access requirements

**Rules & Guidelines:**
- Code of conduct for club members
- Meeting attendance and participation expectations
- Collaboration and contribution standards
- Security practices members must follow
- Consequences for policy violations

**Key Sections Typically Include:**
- **Membership Requirements**: Eligibility and registration process
- **Meeting Schedule**: Regular gathering times and formats
- **Code of Conduct**: Expected behavior and respect policies
- **Security Protocols**: Guidelines for handling sensitive information
- **Contribution Guidelines**: How to propose projects or ideas
- **Resources & Tools**: Approved platforms and learning materials
- **Leadership Roles**: Responsibilities of officers and volunteers

The repository acts as the single source of truth for club governance, ensuring all members understand the expectations and organizational structure. This helps maintain a professional, secure, and welcoming environment for cybersecurity enthusiasts at all skill levels.
